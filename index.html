About Me

Kylee Phillips is a student at Miami University. Her major is Emergency Technology in Business and Design. Within her major, she is interested in design and animation. She is very passionate about art and hopes to one day be an animator. She is from a small town called Plain City, Ohio. She grew up with her two parents and her older sister. She also has two dogs. She graduated from Jonathan Alder in 2023 as a salutatorian. She was involved in many things in high school, such as choir, Spanish Honorary, and art club. Her biggest accomplishment in high school was winning a local art competition her senior year for a self-portrait she did. Her favorite video game is Skyrim. She also loves Red Dead Redemption 2. Her favorite games include ones where the ending is determined by how you play it. She also loves going to concerts and mainly listens to rock music. She has been to around 35 concerts.
